# Open-weight families — high level only

The **market moves monthly**. Exact model names will change before this page is old. What you need is **orientation**:

- **Qwen** (Alibaba family) — often strong multilingual options; check **current** licence on the official site.
- **DeepSeek** — often discussed for reasoning and coding; same rule: **read the licence** today.
- **MiniMax-class** — vendor “mini” stacks; treat as a **category**, not a single product you must use.

**Ollama**, **LM Studio**, **vLLM**, and similar tools are **runners**: they **load** weights and talk to your IDE or terminal. **You** still choose **which** weights and **whether** IT allows them.

## Do this — one bookmark (15 minutes)

1. Pick **one** path you might try later (`ollama.ai`, `lmstudio.ai`, or your package manager docs).
2. Open the **official** getting-started or install page.
3. **Bookmark** it.
4. In **one sentence**, write what the licence allows you to do (personal? commercial? attribution?).
5. If you cannot find a licence link, **stop** — that is a red flag.

> **Do not** download random weight files from forums this week. This course wants **habits**, not a GPU lab on day one.

## Why we stay non-prescriptive

Hardware, drivers, and OS differ. Your **work machine** may block installs. A **Chromebook** may not support local inference. The professional skill is: **find the doc**, **read the licence**, **ask IT** — not memorise a blog post from last month.
